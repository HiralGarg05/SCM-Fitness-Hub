Yoga for Fitness

Hatha Yoga

Hatha yoga focuses on physical postures (asanas), breath control (pranayama), and meditation to improve flexibility, strength, and mindfulness.

Vinyasa Yoga

Vinyasa yoga links breath with movement in a dynamic flow of postures. It enhances strength, flexibility, and mental focus.

Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga is a vigorous style of yoga that follows a specific sequence of postures and incorporates breath control to build strength, flexibility, and stamina.

Kundalini Yoga

Kundalini yoga aims to awaken the dormant spiritual energy (Kundalini) at the base of the spine through chanting, breathing techniques, and postures.

Restorative Yoga

Restorative yoga is a calming and therapeutic form of yoga designed to relax the body and mind through supported poses held for longer periods.

Power Yoga

Power yoga is a faster, high-intensity version of Vinyasa yoga, designed to build strength and endurance while still incorporating flexibility.

Sudarshan Kriya Yoga

Sudarshan Kriya is a powerful and transformative breathing technique that is a key practice in the Art of Living foundation, developed by Sri Sri Ravi Shankar.
