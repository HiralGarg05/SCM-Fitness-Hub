Underweight Diet Plan

To gain weight healthily, increase calorie intake with nutrient-rich foods.

Calories: Increase by 300-500 daily
Protein: 1.2–1.5g per kg
Healthy Fats: 25-30% (nuts, olive oil)
Carbs: 45-50% (whole grains)

Normal Weight Diet Plan

To maintain weight, stick to a balanced diet with appropriate calories.

Calories: Maintain current intake
Protein: 1–1.2g per kg
Healthy Fats: 20-30%
Carbs: 45-50% (fruits, vegetables)

Overweight Diet Plan

Create a slight calorie deficit and focus on nutrient-dense foods.

Calories: Reduce by 300-500
Protein: 1.2–1.5g per kg
Healthy Fats: 20-25%
Carbs: 40-45% (fiber-rich foods)
